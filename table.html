~Interesting ocean animals~

Animal Key Fact Habitat Range
Blue Whale The largest animal on Earth, reaching up to 100 feet in length. Found in all the world's oceans, p articularly in deep and open waters.
Great White Sharka Apex predator known for its powerful bite and keen sense of smell. Coastal and offshore waters, typically in temperate and subtropical regions.
Dolphin Highly intelligent and social, known for their playful behavior. Most oceans and seas worldwide, from tropical to temperate waters.
Giant Squid One of the largest invertebrates, with eyes as large as dinner plates. Deep ocean waters, typically at depths of 300 to 1000 meters.
Sea Turtle Ancient reptiles that have existed for over 100 million years. Tropical and subtropical oceans, along coastal areas and open sea.
Clownfish Known for their symbiotic relationship with sea anemones Warm waters of the Pacific and Indian Oceans, among coral reefs.
Octopus Known for their intelligence, ability to camouflage, and escape artistry Ocean floors, coral reefs, and coastal waters worldwide.
Manta Ray Graceful swimmers with wingspans that can reach up to 23 feet. Tropical and subtropical ocean waters, often near coral reefs.
Seahorse Unique for their horse-like shape and the males' role in pregnancy. Shallow coastal waters, seagrass beds, and coral reefs worldwide.
Jellyfish Possess tentacles with stinging cells and have been around for millions of years. Found in every ocean, from the surface to the deep sea.
